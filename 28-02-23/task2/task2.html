SunSet Walk

Brand New Sunset Walk (off US 192) This brand new development and resort is just off the US192. The Mini Town Centre includes bars, restaurants, outside entertainment, street exhibitions, some market stalls, Sketchers outlet, TJ Max, Ross, Ice cream…..Water Park and loads of Slides… and much more…..well worth a visit as only 10 minutes drive away..

Florence Villa

Directions to Sunset Walk Turn right out of Hillcrest Drive onto Florence Villa Grove Road and you will reach Highway 27 less than 900 yards away. Turn right onto Highway 27; this is a dual carriageway. In 5 minutes you will reach US192 and follow the signs for Disney World, going straight ahead through numerous traffic lights. After about 4 miles take the right turn signed for Sunset Walk. There is free car parking.
